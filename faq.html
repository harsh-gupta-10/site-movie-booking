How do I book a movie ticket on your website?

To book a movie ticket on our website, you need to first select the movie you want to watch, choose the date and time that suits you, select your preferred seats and then proceed to payment.

What are the payment options available on your website?

We accept payments through debit/credit cards, net banking, and various mobile wallets

Can I cancel my movie ticket booking and get a refund?

Yes, you can cancel your booking and get a refund before the showtime. However, cancellation charges may apply.

How do I know if my booking is confirmed?

Once you complete the booking process and make the payment, you will receive a confirmation email and/or SMS with your booking details.

Is there a convenience fee for booking tickets online?

Yes, a convenience fee may be charged for booking tickets online. This fee covers the cost of providing the online booking service.

Can I select my seats while booking tickets?

Yes, you can select your preferred seats while booking tickets. The seat selection is subject to availability.

How early can I book tickets for a movie?

You can book tickets as soon as the movie is listed on our website. Some theaters allow bookings up to a week in advance.
